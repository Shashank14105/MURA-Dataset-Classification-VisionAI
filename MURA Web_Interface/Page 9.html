Localization & Focus

Grad-CAM (Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping) highlights regions that heavily influence the model's prediction. The red "hotspots" confirm the model is focusing on the fractured bone rather than background artifacts.

Building Clinical Trust

By visualizing the decision process, radiologists can verify that the AI's reasoning aligns with medical knowledge, transforming the model from a "black box" into a transparent assistant.

Error Analysis

Visualizations help detect overfitting. For example, if the heatmap focuses on a "metal tag" instead of the bone, it indicates the model is learning the wrong features.
